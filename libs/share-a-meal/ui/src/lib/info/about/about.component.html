About Share-A-Meal

Share-A-Meal is a project aimed at reducing food waste and helping those in need. Our platform connects restaurants and grocery stores with local charities and food banks to donate excess food that would otherwise go to waste. By doing so, we hope to make a positive impact on both the environment and our community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a more sustainable and equitable food system by reducing food waste and providing access to nutritious food for those in need.

How It Works

Share-A-Meal works by connecting local restaurants and grocery stores with nearby charities and food banks. When a restaurant or grocery store has excess food that would otherwise go to waste, they can use our platform to quickly and easily donate it to a local charity or food bank. Charities and food banks can then distribute the donated food to those in need.

Our Impact

Since our launch in 2020, Share-A-Meal has helped to reduce food waste and provide access to nutritious food for thousands of people in need. We are proud to have partnered with over 100 local restaurants and grocery stores, and to have donated over 50,000 pounds of food to local charities and food banks.

Get Involved

If you're interested in getting involved with Share-A-Meal, there are a number of ways you can help. You can donate to our cause, volunteer at a local charity or food bank, or spread the word about our platform to your friends and family. Together, we can create a more sustainable and equitable food system for all.
